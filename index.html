Welcome to EduKids!

A Smart Learning Platform for Kids

“Let the kids no longer be dependent on Pen and Paper to learn, read and write.”

Key Features

Air Pen

Air Pen and Smart Canvas are the latest tools which detect hand gestures in the air, enabling kids to visualize and write without pen and paper. Let your kids learn Alphabets and Number by writing in the air with their fingers using this fun and enjoyable tool!




Virtual Keyboard

Experience the joy of typing in air with the help of virtual keyboard, where your kid can learn to recognize objects and type their names, perform addition and subtraction and fill in the blanks- all with just their fingers.

Unlock Characters

Complete activities daily and unlock new characters as soon as you get enough points! This feature ensures your kid makes progress continuously.
